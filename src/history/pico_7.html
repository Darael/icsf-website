Picocon 7 - 1989

The guests for this Picocon were Dave Langford, Terry Pratchett, Gerry Webb and Dave Lally.... according to one source. According to the Felix article, it was Bob Shaw not Gerry Webb. Ah well. According to Simon Bradshaw "It went wrong. Actually it probably went ok, but owing to various hassles it was a big panic behind the scenes." (sounds familiar)

There was also a talk from the Amateur Rocketry Group, wargaming (Paranoia and Space:1889) and videos all day.
